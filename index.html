Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 6, 2026

At Naelo, we believe your thoughts should be for your eyes only. Our privacy philosophy is simple: we don't want your data, so we don't collect it.

1. Data Storage

All journal entries, images, and settings created within Naelo are stored locally on your device. We do not maintain any cloud servers that store your personal content. If you delete the app without a backup, your data is gone forever—exactly the way a private journal should be.

2. Biometric Security

When you enable Privacy Lock, Naelo uses Apple's FaceID or TouchID systems. This authentication happens entirely at the system level. Naelo never sees or stores your biometric data; we only receive a "success" or "failure" signal from the operating system.

3. No Tracking

We do not use third-party analytics that track your individual behavior or identify you. We believe in a clean, distraction-free environment that respects your digital footprint.

4. Third-Party Services

Naelo does not share your data with third parties. Your journals are yours alone.
